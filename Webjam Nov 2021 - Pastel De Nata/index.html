Experience Tradition

The pastel de nata is a Portuguese icon, and with good reason- this tasty pastry has been part of the culture since the 18th century. The egg custard tart has since spread internationally, with cafes serving them worldwide from London, to the United States, even Japan.


Experience tradition by finding a bakery that sells them close to you, or try something new in the kitchen and make them yourself!
