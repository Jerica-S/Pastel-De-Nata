How To Make

Use this easy to follow recipe to create pastel de nata!

Pastry

  • 290g plain flour
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 210ml water
  • 240g unsalted butter, softened
  • extra flour for dusting

Custard

  • 40g all-purpose flour
  • 50ml milk
  • 165ml water
  • 240g white sugar
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Additional 250ml milk
